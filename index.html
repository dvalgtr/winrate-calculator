How Does It Work?

This calculator uses a mathematical formula to determine how many consecutive wins you need to reach your desired winrate.

The formula used:

Wins Needed = (Desired Winrate * Total Matches - Current Wins) / (1 - Desired Winrate)

Where Current Wins = Current Winrate * Total Matches / 100
